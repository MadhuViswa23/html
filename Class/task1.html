Nature refers to the physical world and all its phenomena, including the Earth's ecosystems, landscapes, living organisms, and natural processes. It encompasses the natural environment, untouched by human intervention, and exists independently of human society. Nature exhibits remarkable diversity, ranging from lush forests, majestic mountains, and vast oceans to intricate ecosystems supporting a wide array of flora and fauna
