• Butterfly

The butterfly is a beautiful and delicate insect known for its vibrant colors and graceful flight. It undergoes a remarkable transformation, from a caterpillar to a pupa and then to a butterfly. With its intricate patterns and wings, it captivates people of all ages. Butterflies play an essential role in pollination and contribute to the ecosystem's balance. Their presence adds a touch of magic and wonder to nature, making them a beloved symbol of beauty and transformation. Marine ecosystems cover around 70% of the Earth's surface and are vital for sustaining life. They provide habitat for a diverse range of marine organisms, from microscopic plankton to majestic whales. Coral reefs, mangroves, and kelp forests are crucial marine habitats. Conservation efforts are essential to protect these valuable ecosystems from pollution and overfishing.
